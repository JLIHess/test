Hotel and Transportation

Atlanta Evergreen Marritt
Conference Resort

4021 Lakeview Drive
Stone Mountain, Georgia

Hotel

Surround yourself with the beauty of Stone Mountain, GA at Atlanta Evergreen Marriott Conference Resort. Set within Stone Mountain Park – Georgia’s most visited attraction – The hotel offers newly renovated guestrooms with private balconies offering nature, pool, or lake views. Take a dip in the heated indoor or outdoor pool, work out in the 24/7 gym, or practice your swing on the 36-hole golf course. The lakefront conference center boasts 64,000 sq. ft. of indoor and outdoor venue space.
