Programs

The final daily schedule will be posted just prior to the retreat. You can view this year's growing faculty list by clicking here. The general structure: times for meals, workshops, events, entertainments and banquet is similar each year. You may view past years' program guides here.

Group babysitting for babies and toddlers under age 2 is available for $100/day ($500 for the entire Retreat) from 9 a.m. through dinner. Private babysitting is available, with advance notice, after dinner for $15/hour. Please email info@jretreat.com to reserve.

The National Jewish Retreat offers an exciting youth recreational program for children and teenagers ages 2–13 included in the children’s registration fee. Part of the international network of Camp Gan Israel summer camps, our warm and friendly staff will provide children with a fun experience that will incorporate Jewish themes so children can share the joy of learning that marks the Retreat. Registrants who cancel on or before July 15, 2021 the National Jewish Retreat will refund the entire registration cost minus a $75.00 administration fee per person. Registrants who cancel reservations on July 15, 2021 through July 29, 2021 will be refunded 50% of the registration cost minus the $75.00 fee. There will be no refunds on cancellation made on or after July 30, 2021. Cancellation must be made via email to info@jretreat.com. Cancellation fees are tax-deductible contributions to JLI. Reservations are non-transferable.

Inside the hotel, the air conditioning can be cool, so be sure to bring along something to layer on. Formal attire is suggested for Thursday evening's banquet as well as Friday evening and Shabbat.

There will be a singles orientation on Tuesday evening for singles to get acquainted with one another. Your registration will also have the option for you to choose to be seated with other singles during meals with assigned seating.

Providing a safer environment for our Retreat guests and presenters is our top priority. This year’s retreat is open to adults who have either been fully vaccinated or have tested positive for Covid -19 within the past 6 months. Children ages 12 and under need not be vaccinated. Consistent with the most recent CDC guidance, Retreat participants will not be required to wear face coverings or social distance in indoor or outdoor areas of the hotel.
